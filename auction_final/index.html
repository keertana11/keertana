Online Auction System is an online platform where a user will be able to post items online for auction. The items will accompany item name, selling price, and a picture presentation for the bidder to see. The bidder, if interested in the item, will auction for the product and will be able to inspect the item physically to approve the product then complete the business with the seller. This is important since the auctioneer does not necessarily need to make a physical consultation with the seller for him/her to get the required services....
